## Blog Post Outline Example

**Title:** "Top 10 Cosmos Hub Validators by Delegator Count — March 2026"

**Structure:**
1. Intro: Why delegator count matters (social proof, trust signal)
2. Methodology: Data source (our DB), snapshot date, criteria
3. Top 10 table: Moniker, delegator count, tokens, commission, uptime
4. Analysis: Patterns (concentration, commission trends, new entrants)
5. How to compare: Link to /comparevalidators
6. CTA: Check your validator's stats at validatorinfo.com

**Tone:** Data-first, no opinions on which validator is "best"
