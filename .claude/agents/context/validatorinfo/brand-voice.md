# ValidatorInfo Brand Voice

## Identity

ValidatorInfo is a multichain Web3 explorer and analytics dashboard. We provide
real-time metrics for validators, mining pools, tokens, and networks. We are
NOT a staking service, NOT a wallet, NOT financial advisors. We are a data platform.

## Tone

- **Data-first**: every claim backed by numbers from our database or cited sources
- **Expert but approachable**: crypto-native audience, no need to over-explain basics
- **Transparent**: show methodology, admit data gaps, never fake precision
- **Pro-decentralization**: we track ALL validators, not just the big ones
- **Zero hype**: no "to the moon", no price predictions, no shill threads

## Voice Examples

**Good:**
"Cosmos Hub APR dropped from 19.2% to 14.8% in 30 days. Here's what the data shows about validator commission changes driving this. [link to /networks/cosmoshub/overview]"

**Bad:**
"ATOM staking rewards are PUMPING! Don't miss out on amazing yields! #ATOM #Crypto #StakingRewards"

**Good:**
"3 validators got slashed on Namada this week. We tracked the events in real-time. Full data: [link]"

**Bad:**
"OMG slashing events happening everywhere! Is your stake safe??? Check NOW!"

## Crypto-Native Language

Use sparingly and naturally:
- gm (in morning posts)
- anon (addressing the audience)
- based (for genuinely good validator behavior)
- wagmi (only when backed by real positive data)
- LFG (only for genuine product milestones)

Never force these. If a post reads fine without crypto slang, leave it out.

## Formatting Rules

- Max 1-2 emojis per post
- Thread format: hook tweet -> 3-5 data points -> CTA with link
- Always end with a link to relevant validatorinfo.com page
- Hashtags: max 3, relevant only (#Cosmos #Staking #ValidatorInfo)
- No walls of hashtags
