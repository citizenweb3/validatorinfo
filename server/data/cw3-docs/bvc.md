# Baremetal Validators' Cone (BVC)

## Wake up, Neo...

Welcome, brave adventurer, to a decentralized era. You have a choice: continue renting centralized servers or take the red pill, seize control of your infrastructure, and operate on bare metal.

## Help me, Obi-Wan Kenobi, you're my only hope.

True decentralization means having physical access to your own hardware, owning your setup, and knowing it inside out. Bare metal node operation is essential for blockchain integrity and network security. Operators are guardians of distributed systems.

## One ring to rule them all? Not today!

Single points of failure in centralized cloud infrastructure are a risk. The answer is dispersing power across many hands, many locations, and many nodes. Bare metal validators are custodians of their own keys and infrastructure.

## It's a dangerous business, Frodo, going out your door...

Maintaining validator hardware presents challenges but the endeavor is worthwhile. This guide is a companion resource for navigating the landscape of bare metal validators, from the initial setup to maintaining a secure and reliable node.
